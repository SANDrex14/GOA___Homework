FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers coding challenges to help developers improve their HTML, CSS, and JavaScript skills.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes, it offers both free and premium coding challenges.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Yes, you can showcase completed projects in your portfolio.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

You can ask questions in the Frontend Mentor community or join discussions on Discord.
